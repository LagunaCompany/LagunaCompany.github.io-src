The Arcadia Group

If you have used any of The Arcadia Group's eCommerce websites over the last five years, it's almost certain you have interacted with something I built. I write CMS templates that the brands (including Topshop and Burton) use to create their home pages and e-mails, and I implement styling changes based on their designs.

Being a senior developer there, it's also my responsibility to advise the brands about the feasibility of their development requests, and to offer delivery estimates.

Other items in this portfolio will outline specific projects I worked on for Arcadia.

  • Client: The Arcadia Group Ltd
  • Date: May 2010 - Present
  • Service: Web Development

Topshop Main Shoot Feature

Built using a CMS template I wrote based on the Isotope plugin, complete with a fluid/responsive layout. I also sourced a plug–in to handle responsive text; the plug–in detects the window size, applies a base font size to a container, and relative font sizes on child elements ensure the text retains its proportions in relation to the images.

  • Client: Topshop
  • Date: March 2015
  • Service: Web Development
